Free Store Club Room 264

The offical website of NWHS Free Store Club

We are the Northwest High School Free Store Club. Located in room 264, we are a non-profit club that serves as an outlet for storing and giving out supplies and items. We take in all types of donations! Feel free to drop by Room 264 and donate or drop off any items!

Who Are We

Who are the Free Store Club?

The Free Store Club, is an off-branch of the Green Club, founded by _______. ________ is a English teacher from Northwest High School that runs mutiple clubs that benefits students in the school's community. We strive to redistribute unwanted items and supplies to anybody who needs them.

How we Operate

The Free Store Club takes in donations from across the school and community to organize and put them up for anybody to take. Many donations such as notebooks come in as used, and we renew those supplies to prevent them from being thrown out.

During the school year, we set up malls and giveaways for various types of items. We host annual giveaways on jewelry and school supplies!
